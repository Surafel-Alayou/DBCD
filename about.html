About Us

DBCD (Debre Birhan Community Development) is a charitable organization dedicated to improving the lives of the people in Debre Birhan City through various initiatives. The organization is made up of individuals residing in the USA who share a common goal of contributing to the development of their mother city. DBCD focuses on creating educational opportunities, improving healthcare, promoting community thinking, and creating job opportunities in Debre Birhan. In addition, they work alongside a sister charity called Fikir Debre Birhan Community Development to maximize their impact. Through these efforts, DBCD and its members are committed to making a meaningful difference in the lives of the people of Debre Birhan.
